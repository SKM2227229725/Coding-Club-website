The Coding Club at Community for Technology and Coding (CTC) is a vibrant community of passionate developers, programmers, and tech enthusiasts. We aim to cultivate a strong coding culture by organizing workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects. Our goal is to empower students to learn, innovate, and excel in the world of technology.
